ABOUT GRAND ROYAL HOTEL

The hotel industry like another business opens up socio-economic opportunities for both the owner and customer. It has the function of providing hospitality services to customers. These customers can be travelers, foreigners, business people, tourists or just visitors. Customers are mostly constrained in trying to get a room to spend the night, in as the usual practice is to look for a hotel when you have arrived at the location, walk in and find out if there is vacant room. If there is no vacant room, you have to look for another hotel to see if there is vacancy. Sometimes you might get the contact number of a certain hotel to reach them and book a room. But do the hotel attendants really ensure to keep a room for you?. You would be lucky to go and get a room booked for you. They usually serve those who walk in rather than who may get access to them through phone number.so the online Reservation system helps in solving such problems. The importance of computer and its application have been recognized globally.The impact of computers to the society have been felt in hotels' booking system.Computer booking system has brought a lot of change improvement in hotel efficiency in hotel operation. With modern technology,hotels can strengthen their functionality by managing and processing online services.This online service will help the clients book hotel rooms from the comfort of their homes and they can also plan their budget very well as long as the hotel has given them all the information needed about the reservation. Computer booking system has brought a lot of change improvement and efficiency in hotel operation. There is a great need to get easy, quick and organized information system about a certain hotel by the traveler so that they can plan their vacation accordingly. Through an online hotel reservation system, hoteliers and travelers will both be benefited. Hotels will have minimal need of agents to travel the world to look for clients because information about their hotel is available online. They also help the client for they will just go the online website of the hotel and enquire about any information they want concerning the hotel. Originally online booking system were created for airline travels, but with time they became great use to hotels.
